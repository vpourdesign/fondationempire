Why the Polycrete System in Mont-Tremblant

Foundations built for the Mont-Tremblant climate

  • Deep frost and freeze-thaw cycles In Mont-Tremblant, ground frost can reach more than 5 feet deep. The permanent R-25 or R-29 insulation of the Polycrete System protects the concrete from the freeze-thaw cycles that weaken conventional foundations.
  • Structural strength for mountain terrain Rocky soil, slopes and high water tables — the continuous reinforced concrete core and the sustained lateral pressure rating of 75 kPa guarantee stability, even on the region's most demanding sites.
  • Heating savings in Northern climates With six-month winters and temperatures dropping below -30°C, the R-25 to R-29 thermal envelope of the Polycrete System significantly reduces energy costs — for homes, chalets and commercial buildings alike.
  • Local expertise and compliance Fondations Empire is based in Mont-Tremblant (1098 St-Jovite Street). Our team knows the local soils, the standards of the MRC des Laurentides (regional county) and the requirements of the Quebec Building Authority (RBQ).
