Our advantages

Why choose the Polycrete System?

  • Effective R-25 or R-29 insulation Expanded polystyrene panels on both sides of the concrete core — significant heating cost reduction in northern climates.
  • 4-hour fire resistance Continuous reinforced concrete core meeting Building Code requirements for structural separations.
  • STC 52+ sound rating Acoustic comfort markedly superior to traditional wood-frame construction.
  • 96 in × 24 in block — 16 sq ft per panel Twice the length of a conventional ICF block: fewer seams, fewer manipulations, a significantly faster jobsite.
  • Sustained lateral pressure of 75 kPa Pour stability (1,600 psf) — minimal bracing, efficient concrete pour sequencing.
  • Built-in steel structure Continuous steel mesh and integral steel furring strip — superior rigidity during pour and compatibility with all common exterior claddings.
  • Factory pre-cut Faster install, less jobsite waste.

Our competitive edge

Fewer seams. Greater strength. Faster install.

96 in
Block length

Eight feet per panel — twice the length of a conventional ICF block. The result: fewer seams to seal, fewer jobsite manipulations, and a wall that goes up significantly faster.

Steel
Internal steel structure

Continuous steel mesh built into the panel — superior rigidity during pour, no plastic ties to replace, and a metal furring strip compatible with all exterior cladding.
